# WILDER WORLD — MARKETING PLAN
### Executive One-Pager | March 2026

---

## THE PROBLEM

Wilder World needs 10,000 DAU within 90 days. Traditional paid UA costs $5–15/install with poor retention. The game has stunning visuals and compelling gameplay — but nobody is seeing it. The team can't produce enough content internally to feed TikTok, Instagram, YouTube, and X daily at the volume needed to break through.

---

## THE SOLUTION: THE WILDER CONTENT MACHINE

A self-sustaining flywheel that turns players into content creators, content creators into marketers, and $WILD into the fuel that powers it all — at 10–15× cheaper than paid ads.

---

## THE THREE ENGINES

### 🎮 ENGINE 1 — WILDER STREAM (Source Content)
**What:** Players stream & record WW gameplay, upload to stream.wilderworld.com
**Incentive:** $WILD payouts based on Stream Quality Score (resolution × duration × action density × concurrent viewers)
**Output:** Hundreds of hours of high-res gameplay footage per week — the raw material
**Target payout:** ~$50–100/hr equivalent for a 1K CCV 4K streamer (doubles their organic Twitch income)
**Month 1 budget:** $15,000 → ~150 hours of quality footage

### ✂️ ENGINE 2 — WILDER CLIPS (Distribution)
**What:** Clippers browse the Wilder Stream content library, edit short-form clips, post on TikTok/IG/Shorts
**Incentive:** $WILD per view on their unique trackable link (progressive CPM: $0.22–$2.20 per 1K views)
**Output:** Thousands of short-form clips across every platform, every week
**Target:** 33M organic views/month at ~$0.60 effective CPM
**Month 1 budget:** $20,000 → ~33M views

### 📢 ENGINE 3 — WILDER SOCIALS (Official Channels)
**What:** The best clips from Engine 2 get reposted on official WW channels (X, TikTok, IG, YouTube)
**Incentive:** 3× multiplier for any clip featured on official channels
**Output:** Daily content across all WW socials without internal production
**Channels:**
- **Daily:** Wilder Everydays (top clip of the day)
- **Monday:** This Week in Wilder World (best clips compilation)
- **Tuesday:** Dev Log (team + community content)
- **Wednesday:** Game Night highlights
- **Thursday:** Wilder Supply Drop (new items + gameplay showcase)
- **Friday:** Community Playtest clips
